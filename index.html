Guide For Junior Software Engineers

Tips on how to become organised & productive

Build a Strong Portfolio:

Showcase your best projects on a personal website or platforms like GitHub. Highlight skills and technologies that are in demand.

Set Clear Boundaries:

Define your working hours, communication preferences, and project scope upfront to avoid misunderstandings and burnout.

Invest in Continuous Learning:

Keep up with new tools, frameworks, and industry trends to stay competitive and offer more value to your clients.
